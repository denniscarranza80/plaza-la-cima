Our Story

Made for
Southwest Little Rock.

Plaza La Cima was built around one idea: give this neighborhood a place to eat, shop, and get things done, without driving across town.

The Vision

A plaza that grows with its neighbors.

Stagecoach Road is one of the fastest-growing corridors in Southwest Little Rock. Plaza La Cima was designed to meet that growth head-on — bringing together a mix of dining, retail, and service tenants that reflect the community around them.

Every unit is leased with intention: real businesses, run by real people, building something lasting in a plaza designed to be the heart of the block.
